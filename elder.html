ELDER'S WELFARE

Health Care Facilities

  • Integration of telehealth services to provide remote medical consultations and monitoring, especially for seniors with limited mobility or living in remote areas.
  • Case management to ensure seamless communication and collaboration among healthcare providers for comprehensive care.
  • Access to regular health check-ups and medical care,specialized healthcare services catering to age-related conditions.
  • Opportunities for elders to engage in volunteer work,community outreach programs to involve seniors in social initiatives.

Aware To Goverenment Scheme

  • Establishing dedicated pension clinics where individuals can visit to receive personalized assistance in understanding and applying for pension benefits.
  • Offering ongoing support for pension beneficiaries to ensure they continue to receive their entitlements and addressing any issues that may arise.
  • Organizing community workshops specifically focused on explaining the details of pension schemes available, including eligibility criteria, application procedures, and the benefits offered.
  • Provide informational pamphlets, brochures, and application forms during these campaigns, addressing any questions or concerns.

Nutrition and Meals services

  • Nutritious meal programs, including home-delivered meals for those unable to cook,dietary support for specific health conditions.
  • Nutrition and meals are vital components of elder welfare, ensuring the well-being of the elderly population.
  • Access to well-balanced and nutritious meals is essential to support their physical health, immune function, and overall quality of life.
  • Nutrition programs for seniors often consider dietary restrictions and health conditions, providing tailored meal plans.
